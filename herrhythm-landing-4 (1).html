Every week of your cycle your hormones, metabolism, hunger, and water retention change. Generic apps don't know that. HerRhythm does.

🌱
Week 1
Menstrual

Inflammation drops, energy is low. Your body is resetting. Weight often falls here — this is your true baseline.

☀️
Week 2
Follicular

Estrogen rises. Energy peaks. Your best week for harder workouts, lower appetite, and steady weight loss.

✨
Week 3
Ovulation

Brief water retention spike is normal and temporary. Metabolism is slightly elevated. Don't panic at the scale.

🌙
Week 4
Luteal

Progesterone dominates. Hunger surges, cravings hit hard, water retention peaks. This is biology — not failure.
