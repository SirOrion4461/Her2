ℹ️ What to know
Hormonal BC suppresses your natural cycle. You won't have a true period — withdrawal bleeds on the pill are different from a natural period. HerRhythm tracks your symptom patterns over time so you can identify if your BC method is affecting your mood, weight, libido, or energy. Spotting, mood changes, and low libido are common BC side effects worth logging.

Your hormones are shifting. HerRhythm helps you understand what's changing and why.
🌊 What's Happening
Perimenopause typically begins in your 40s (sometimes late 30s) and can last 2–10 years. Estrogen production becomes irregular — surging and dropping unpredictably. Cycles may shorten, lengthen, or become irregular. The hallmark is 12+ consecutive months without a period marking the transition to menopause. Tracking your symptoms over time reveals your personal hormone pattern.
