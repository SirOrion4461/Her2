✨ Ovulation (D14–16)↓ 150–250 cal deficit
🌙 Luteal (D17–28)↑ 100–150 cal above cut
During the luteal phase your metabolism runs ~150–300 cal/day faster. Eating at a severe deficit in this phase raises cortisol, increases muscle breakdown, and worsens PMS. A smaller deficit (or maintenance) in the luteal phase protects muscle and preserves hormonal health — and still produces fat loss when combined with follicular-phase cuts.

Phase Training Guide for Athletes
Menstrual Phase
Days 1–5
🏋️Lower intensity. Focus on mobility, yoga, light lifting. Your body is resetting.
🥩Prioritize iron-rich foods. Blood loss depletes iron — critical for oxygen transport in athletes.
🎯Cut strategy: Eat at maintenance. Severe cuts during menstruation increase cortisol and delay recovery.
Follicular Phase
Days 6–13
⚡Peak strength window. Your best week for PRs, heavy lifts, HIIT, and high-volume training.
🔥Insulin sensitivity is high — carbs are used efficiently. Perfect time for carb-fueled performance.
🎯Cut strategy: Largest deficit here. Body handles caloric restriction best. Aim for 300–500 cal below TDEE.
💪Bulk strategy: Best muscle synthesis window. Extra calories go to muscle, not fat, during this phase.
Ovulation Phase
Days 14–16
🏃Energy and coordination are high. Great for speed work, sport-specific training, and competition.
⚠️Estrogen peak increases ligament laxity — slightly higher ACL/injury risk. Warm up thoroughly.
🎯Cut strategy: Moderate deficit 150–250 cal. Maintain performance while still in a cut.
Luteal Phase
Days 17–28
🌡️Core body temp is elevated ~0.5–1°F. Endurance performance may feel harder. Allow for this.
💧Increased water retention (2–5 lbs). This is not fat — don't panic at the scale or training weight.
🥩Protein and fat needs increase. Cravings are real — your metabolism is running faster. Feed it well.
🎯Cut strategy: Pull back to 100–150 cal deficit max, or maintenance. Deep cuts here cause muscle loss, cortisol spikes, and worsen PMS/performance.
💪Bulk strategy: Surplus works well here — elevated appetite makes eating more natural. Keep protein high.
